Typically 5-10 minutes for strips vs 30-45 minutes for pills. Strips dissolve on your tongue and absorb through your mouth. No waiting for pills to break down, no losing potency to stomach acid.*

Will energy strips make me jittery?

No jitters, no crash. We pair caffeine with L-theanine – a natural amino acid derived from green tea that smooths out the energy boost. It's why our strips give you focus without anxiety, energy without the afternoon crash. Many customers who can't handle coffee love our strips.
