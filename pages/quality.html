Our Testing Protocol

Every batch of Wingman strips goes through a rigorous multi-stage testing protocol:

  1. Raw Material Testing: All ingredients are tested for identity, purity, and potency before production begins.
  2. In-Process Testing: We monitor critical quality attributes throughout the manufacturing process.
  3. Finished Product Testing: Each batch is tested for dissolution, content uniformity, and microbiological safety.
  4. Third-Party Verification: Independent labs verify our results before any batch is released.
  5. Stability Testing: We conduct ongoing stability testing to ensure quality throughout shelf life.
