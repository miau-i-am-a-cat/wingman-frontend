Accessibility Statement

We are committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We continually improve the user experience for everyone and apply relevant accessibility standards.

Our Commitment

Wingman Labs is committed to ensuring that our website is accessible to people with disabilities. We strive to adhere to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA standards.

We are actively working to increase the accessibility and usability of our website and in doing so adhere to many of the available standards and guidelines.

Accessibility Features

We have implemented the following features to improve accessibility:

  • Semantic HTML structure for screen reader compatibility
  • Alternative text for images
  • Keyboard navigation support
  • Color contrast that meets WCAG standards
  • Resizable text without loss of functionality
  • Clear and consistent navigation
  • Skip navigation links
  • Form labels and error messages

Known Issues

We recognize that some areas of our website may not be fully accessible. We are actively working to identify and fix these issues. Known areas for improvement include:

  • Some third-party content may not be fully accessible
  • Some older PDF documents may not be fully accessible
  • Some interactive elements may require keyboard navigation improvements

Contact Us

If you experience any difficulty accessing any part of our website, please contact us. We welcome your feedback and will work to address any accessibility concerns.

Email: team@thewingmanlabs.com

When contacting us about accessibility, please include:

  • The specific page or feature you had difficulty with
  • The assistive technology you use (if any)
  • Your contact information

We will respond to accessibility feedback within 2 business days.
