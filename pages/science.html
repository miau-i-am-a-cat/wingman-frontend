THE SCIENCE OF NOT SWALLOWING YOUR SUPPLEMENTS

Up to 90% of swallowed supplements never reach circulation. They're destroyed by stomach acid, metabolized by the liver, or simply pass through your system. Oral strips change everything.

Bioavailability Matters

Bioavailability is the percentage of an ingredient that actually reaches your bloodstream. Higher bioavailability means more effect per dose.

Traditional Pills

~15%

Pills must survive stomach acid, be absorbed through the gut, and pass through the liver before reaching circulation. Most of the active ingredient is destroyed along the way.

Oral Strips

Up to 90%

Strips dissolve on your tongue and absorb through the mucosal membrane, entering your bloodstream directly. No acid, no first-pass metabolism, no waste.

How Oral Strips Work

1

Dissolve

Place the strip on your tongue. It dissolves in approximately 30 seconds, releasing active ingredients into your saliva.

2

Absorb

Ingredients absorb through the sublingual and buccal membranes in your mouth, bypassing the digestive system entirely.

3

Feel

Active compounds enter your bloodstream directly, so you feel effects in minutes instead of waiting 30-45 minutes.
