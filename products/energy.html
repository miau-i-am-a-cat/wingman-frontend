Mechanism of Action

Caffeine works as an adenosine receptor antagonist in the central nervous system. Adenosine typically promotes sleepiness, so blocking these receptors helps maintain alertness and wakefulness.

Why This Dose

50mg provides alertness equivalent to about half a cup of coffee. This moderate dose is designed to enhance energy without causing jitters or crashes often associated with higher doses.

Source Quality

Extracted from green coffee beans using water extraction methods. This preserves natural compounds while ensuring purity and consistency.

How It Works

L-Theanine is an amino acid that promotes alpha brain wave activity, which is associated with a state of relaxed alertness. It can cross the blood-brain barrier and influence neurotransmitter levels.

Synergy with Caffeine

L-Theanine is known to work synergistically with caffeine, potentially smoothing out caffeine's stimulant effects while maintaining alertness and focus.

Natural Occurrence

Found naturally in tea leaves, particularly green tea. The combination of L-theanine and caffeine in tea is why tea provides a different experience than coffee.

Vitamin B12 is essential for DNA synthesis, red blood cell formation, and neurological function. It plays a crucial role in energy metabolism at the cellular level.

Energy Strip FAQ

Will this replace my morning coffee?

For many people, yes. Each strip has 50mg of caffeine – less than a full cup of coffee – but because it absorbs directly through your mouth and bypasses digestion, users often report feeling it more than their regular coffee. Most people feel it in minutes, not the 30-45 minutes it takes for coffee to kick in. No brewing, no coffee breath, no bathroom lines.

How fast does it work?

The strip dissolves in about 30 seconds and absorbs through your mouth, bypassing your digestive system entirely. Most people feel the effects in minutes – much faster than pills, gummies, or even a cup of coffee that has to travel through your stomach first.

Will I get jitters or a crash?

No jitters, no crash. We pair 50mg caffeine with 30mg L-theanine – a natural amino acid from green tea that smooths out the energy curve. You get calm, focused alertness without the anxiety or afternoon crash. Zero sugar means no blood sugar spike either. Many customers who can't handle coffee love our strips.

How many strips can I take per day?

We recommend starting with 1 strip to gauge your response. The maximum is 4 strips per day (200mg total caffeine). Avoid combining with other caffeine sources like coffee or energy drinks if you're nearing that limit. If you're caffeine-sensitive, stick to 1-2 strips.

What's in it?

50mg natural caffeine (from green tea), 30mg L-theanine, plus Vitamin B12. Zero sugar, zero calories, coffee flavor. Made with plant-based pullulan film. Free from major allergens, gluten, artificial preservatives, and synthetic colors. Made in FDA-registered, GMP-certified facilities.

Who shouldn't use it?

Energy strips aren't for everyone. Avoid if you're: under 18, pregnant or nursing, caffeine-sensitive, or have heart conditions or anxiety disorders. If you're on medications – especially stimulants, sedatives, or antidepressants – consult your doctor first. When in doubt, start with one strip.

How do I use it?

Peel the sachet from the top and place the strip in your mouth. Let it dissolve – takes about 30 seconds. No water needed. Pro tip: place it under your tongue for faster absorption. And if it sticks to the roof of your mouth, don't worry – it will work no matter where in your mouth it sticks to. Works great first thing in the morning, pre-workout, or before a meeting.
